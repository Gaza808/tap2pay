Charging your EV on PayByPhone

Before starting, make sure your PayByPhone app is updated to the latest version in the App Store or Google Play
1/6
Open the app and tap on ‘EV Charging’ to navigate to the charger you need
2/6
When you arrive, select the charger you need. Then tap on ‘charge’ in your app
3/6
View the tariff price and confirm your payment method. Then pay the £30 pre-authorisation fee.
4/6
Tap ‘Start charging’ to begin your charging session. You can check its status any time from the app.
5/6
To end your charging session, use the ‘Slide to end session’ slider in your app. Drive off into the sunset.
6/6
Once finished, you can see your final receipt in the ‘receipts’ section in the app.
