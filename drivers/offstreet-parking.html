Simplifying Off-Street parking

PayByPhone Off-Street Parking Solutions make it easy to pay in off-street public or privately-owned car parks using the same trusted app you already use for on-street parking.
Discover AutoPay and Pay on Exit, two simple options that offer a fast, ticketless, and simplified parking experience.
PayByPhone AutoPayPay on Exit

PayByPhone AutoPay
‍The ultimate hands-free experience

No need to tap, scan or use a payment machine. PayByPhone AutoPay now allows you to open the barriers every time you enter or leave a car park. Our technology uses ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) to detect your number plate and process payments seamlessly.
Download the app nowDownload the app now

How does it work?

1. Opt in to Autopay
in your PayByPhone account for your number plate to be recognized by the ANPR cameras.
2. Park & Enjoy your day
Drive into the AutoPay-enabled car park: the barrier opens automatically.
3. Drive away!
The barrier opens automatically, your parking is calculated and the payment is processed via your preferred payment method in the app.
PayByPhone AutoPay is available in 60 Q-Park parking facilities in England and Scotland, including Liverpool, Manchester, London, Glasgow and Edinburgh. Check all available locations here.

Pay on Exit
No more queuing at a machine!

With Pay on Exit, simply open the app when you're ready to leave the car park and pay only for the time you've parked. It's a seamless experience, all handled from your phone.
