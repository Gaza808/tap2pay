How do I book a garage on PayByPhone?

Before starting, make sure your PayByPhone app is updated to the latest version in the App Store or Google Play
1/5
Open the app and tap on the ‘My cars’ tab on the bottom right of your home screen
2/5
Tap ‘Book MOT’ or ‘Book Service’ on the ‘My cars’ screen to search for a garage (you will need your mileage for Servicing)
3/5
Choose your garage and date, then add or confirm your payment details.
4/5
The garage will be in touch to confirm the booking by the next working day!
5/5
If any additional repairs or work is needed, you’ll need to approve this in the app
